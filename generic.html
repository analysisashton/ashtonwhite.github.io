About Me


Hi there, my name is Ashton White, and I am a recent BSBM graduate from Tennessee Technological University with a concentration in data analytics and business intelligence. I fell in love with data and data storytelling while taking Statistics, Econometrics, Data Analytics, and Business Intelligence courses. I was fascinated by how data is leveraged to identify patterns and trends, uncover insights, and solve complex business problems.

While at university, I was also a member of the Data Science League, a student organization that focused on exploring the world of data. Working alongside my peers on data-related projects, I discovered the power of collaboration and the value of a team-based approach to problem-solving.

Through my coursework and experiences with the Data Science League, I developed a keen eye for detail and a deep appreciation for the importance of data-driven decision-making. I am excited to continue my journey in the world of data and leverage my skills to drive business success through insights and analysis.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me. Please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or opportunities you'd like to discuss.
